ThriftKids Kenya

E-commerce & branding for second-hand kids’ clothing

View Live Site

Project Overview

ThriftKids Kenya is an online store specializing in quality pre-loved children’s clothing. We created a playful, trustworthy brand identity and an intuitive shopping interface to appeal to parents looking for affordable, sustainable options.

Challenges

  • Stand out in a crowded second-hand market
  • Design a seamless, responsive shopping flow
  • Integrate secure payment and inventory management

Goals

  • Craft a friendly, versatile logo & palette
  • Build a mobile-first e-commerce site
  • Ensure clear navigation and easy checkout

Design & Development Process

  1. Research & Ideation: Competitor analysis & moodboarding
  2. Branding: Sketches → vector logo → color & typography
  3. Wireframes: Low-fidelity layouts for key pages
  4. UI Design: High-fidelity mockups in Figma
  5. Implementation: Hand-coded HTML/CSS/JS
  6. Testing & Launch: QA, performance & SEO optimizations
